# SECTION 1: Lost or Stolen Card

**Document ID:** KB-CARD-001 | **Category:** Card Services

## Overview

If your NovaPay Visa Debit Card or NovaPay Mastercard Credit Card is lost, stolen, or misplaced, immediate action is required to prevent unauthorized transactions.

## Immediate Steps

### Step 1: Lock Your Card Instantly

Use the NovaPay Mobile App to temporarily freeze your card:
1. Open the NovaPay app
2. Navigate to Cards → My Cards
3. Select the affected card
4. Tap Lock Card (red padlock icon)
5. Confirm by entering your 6-digit PIN or biometric authentication

Card locking takes effect within 30 seconds. All pending transactions will be declined.

### Step 2: Report to Customer Support

Contact our 24/7 Card Services Hotline:

| Region | Phone Number | Hours |
|--------|--------------|-------|
| United States | 1-800-NOVAPAY (668-2729) | 24/7 |
| United Kingdom | +44 20 7946 0958 | 24/7 |
| European Union | +49 30 1234 5678 | 24/7 |
| Asia Pacific | +65 6789 0123 | 24/7 |

## Fraud Shield Protocol (FSP-7)

Once reported, NovaPay initiates automatic protection:
1. Immediate card deactivation within 60 seconds
2. Transaction monitoring - AI-powered fraud detection reviews last 72 hours
3. Provisional credit for unauthorized charges within 24 hours
4. New card issuance begins automatically

## Zero Liability Policy (ZLP-2024)

Cardholders are not responsible for unauthorized transactions if:
- Card loss/theft is reported within 48 hours
- PIN was not shared with third parties
- Card was not used for prohibited activities

Maximum liability caps:
- Reported within 2 days: $0 liability
- Reported within 60 days: Maximum $50 liability
- Reported after 60 days: Up to $500 liability

## Emergency Card Services

### Digital Emergency Card (DEC)

While waiting for your physical replacement:
1. Log into NovaPay Online Banking
2. Go to Cards → Emergency Services
3. Select Issue Digital Card
4. Card details appear instantly for online purchases
5. Valid for 14 days or until physical card arrives

### Emergency Cash Advance

- Visit any NovaPay partner branch with valid government ID
- Emergency cash limit: $500 per day
- Fee: $25 service charge
- Requires identity verification (KYC Level 2)

# SECTION 5: KYC Verification Policy

**Document ID:** KB-KYC-001 | **Category:** Compliance  
**Regulations:** USA PATRIOT Act, Bank Secrecy Act (BSA), FinCEN Requirements

## What Is KYC?

Know Your Customer (KYC) is a regulatory requirement that ensures NovaPay can verify the identity of all account holders through the Customer Identification Program (CIP) under the USA PATRIOT Act, Section 326.

## Verification Levels

### Level 1: Basic Verification
**Requirements:** Full legal name, Date of birth, Residential address, Social Security Number (SSN) or ITIN, Email, Phone

**Capabilities:** Account opening, Debit card, Direct deposits up to $5,000/month, ACH up to $2,500/month

### Level 2: Enhanced Verification
**Additional Requirements:** Government-issued photo ID, Proof of address (utility bill within 60 days), Selfie verification (liveness check)

**Additional Capabilities:** Wire transfers, Unlimited direct deposits, ACH up to $25,000/month, International transactions

### Level 3: Premium Verification
**Additional Requirements:** Secondary ID, Source of funds documentation, Employment verification, Tax returns

**Additional Capabilities:** Private banking, High-value wires ($100,000+), No transaction limits

## Acceptable Identity Documents

### Primary ID
| Document | Requirements |
|----------|--------------|
| U.S. Passport | Current, undamaged |
| Driver's License | Current, U.S. state issued |
| State ID Card | Current |
| Permanent Resident Card | Form I-551, current |

### Proof of Address
| Document | Requirements |
|----------|--------------|
| Utility Bill | Dated within 60 days |
| Bank Statement | Dated within 60 days |
| Lease Agreement | Current and signed |

## How to Complete KYC

### Mobile App (Fastest)
1. Open NovaPay app → More → Verify Identity
2. Photo ID Upload - Position ID in frame, capture front and back
3. Selfie Verification - Follow prompts, remove glasses
4. Address Verification - Upload proof document
5. Submit for review

Processing Time: 1-5 minutes for automated; 24-48 hours if manual review needed.

# SECTION 6: Travel Notice Guidelines

**Document ID:** KB-TRAVEL-001 | **Category:** Card Services

## Why Set a Travel Notice?

NovaPay's Fraud Detection System (FDS-AI) monitors transaction patterns. When transactions deviate significantly from your pattern, the system may decline transactions or lock your card. A travel notice tells the system to expect transactions from your destinations.

## How to Set a Travel Notice

### Mobile App (Recommended)
1. Open NovaPay app
2. Tap Cards → Travel Notices
3. Select Add Travel Notice
4. Enter: Destinations, Start/End dates, Cards to include
5. Review and submit
6. Receive confirmation number

# SECTION 8: Mobile App Troubleshooting

**Document ID:** KB-APP-001 | **Category:** Digital Banking  
**App Version:** 8.4.2 (iOS) / 8.4.1 (Android)

## Minimum System Requirements
| Platform | Minimum | Recommended |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| iOS | 14.0 | 16.0+ |
| Android | 9.0 | 12.0+ |
| Storage | 150 MB | 300 MB |

## Login Issues

### Cannot Log In - "Invalid username or password"
1. Verify credentials (username is case-sensitive)
2. Reset password via "Forgot Password"
3. Check Caps Lock
4. Clear saved password and re-enter

### Account Locked
Locks after 5 failed attempts:
- Wait 30 minutes for auto-unlock
- Call 1-800-NOVAPAY for immediate unlock

### Biometric Login Not Working
1. Re-enroll biometric in phone Settings
2. Check app permissions for Face ID/Touch ID
3. Clean fingerprint sensor
4. Toggle biometric off/on in app Settings → Security

### 2FA Code Not Received
1. Wait 2 minutes for carrier delays
2. Check cellular signal
3. Tap "Resend code"
4. Try "Call me instead" option
5. Verify phone number in account settings

## App Performance Issues

### App Crashes or Freezes
1. Force close and reopen
2. Restart device
3. Update app from App Store/Play Store
4. Clear cache (Android: Settings → Apps → NovaPay → Clear Cache)
5. Reinstall app

### App Running Slowly
1. Close background apps
2. Check storage (need 500 MB free)
3. Disable battery saver
4. Update operating system

## Mobile Deposit Issues

### Check Image Rejected - "Image quality too low"
- Use flat, dark surface (not white)
- Ensure good lighting, no shadows
- Hold phone parallel to check
- Capture all four corners
- Make sure endorsement signature visible

### Deposit Limits

|--------------|-------|---------|
| Basic | $1,000 | $5,000 |
| Standard | $2,500 | $10,000 |
| Premium | $5,000 | $25,000 |

## Payment and Transfer Issues

### Transfer Failed
Common causes:
1. Insufficient funds (check available balance)
2. Daily limit exceeded
3. Invalid account number
4. Incorrect routing number
5. KYC verification needed

### Zelle Payment Not Received
1. Verify recipient email/phone
2. Confirm recipient enrolled in Zelle
3. New recipients have 14 days to enroll
4. Check recipient's Zelle settings

# SECTION 9: Account Security Best Practices

**Document ID:** KB-SEC-001 | **Category:** Security

## Password Security

### Creating Strong Passwords
Requirements:
- Minimum 12 characters
- One uppercase (A-Z)
- One lowercase (a-z)
- One number (0-9)
- One special character (!@#$%^&*)

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

### Options
| Method | Security Level |
|--------|----------------|
| Authenticator App | Highest |
| SMS Text Message | High |
| Email Code | Medium |
| Security Key (FIDO2) | Highest |

### Setting Up Authenticator
1. Download Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or Authy
2. In NovaPay: Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication
3. Select "Authenticator App"
4. Scan QR code
5. Enter 6-digit code to verify
6. Save backup codes securely

## Recognizing Fraud

### Phishing Red Flags
- Emails from non @novapay.com addresses
- Urgency messages ("Account will be closed!")
- Requests for password or full card number
- Links to non-novapay.com websites
- Poor grammar or spelling

### NovaPay Will NEVER:
- Ask for your full password
- Request PIN via email/text/phone
- Ask for full Social Security Number
- Send links to "verify your account"

## Device Security

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---------|----------------|
| Screen lock | 6-digit PIN minimum |
| Auto-lock | 30 seconds to 1 minute |
| Software updates | Install immediately |
| App downloads | Official stores only |

## If Account Compromised

1. Change password immediately
2. Enable/reset 2FA
3. Lock all cards
4. Review recent transactions
5. Remove unknown devices
6. Contact support
7. File report if identity theft suspected
